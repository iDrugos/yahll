# Developer Roadmap Guide

## Overview
Structured, community-driven learning paths for 70+ developer roles and technologies. Visual roadmaps show what to learn, in what order, and how concepts connect.

## When to Use
- Onboarding a new developer and need to recommend a learning path
- You're unsure what to learn next in your specialization
- Planning a career transition (e.g., backend → DevOps)
- Advising on skills needed for a specific job role

## How to Use a Roadmap
1. Find your current role/target role
2. Mark what you already know
3. Identify gaps — focus on the **critical path** first (required items, not optional)
4. Pick 1-2 things to learn at a time, not the whole map
5. Build something with each new skill before moving on

## Common Mistake
Treating the roadmap as a checklist to complete sequentially. It's a map — pick your route based on your goals, not a prescription to learn everything.
